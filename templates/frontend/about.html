Our Story

Set beside the verdant greenery, lush vegetation and the spectacular Koggala Lake, Chari Lake offers an experience that is like no other. Just a 90 minute drive from Colombo it can also be accessed in 15 minutes by a domestic flight to Koggala. This contemporary boutique hotel consists of 10 exquisitely designed rooms and 2 suites. Elegantly designed with timber and polished cement structures this eco-destination offers an experience that will give you a sense of satisfaction and sanctity. The boutique is opulent in design and offers a life changing experience. Luxurious elegance overlooking the Koggala Lake is a sight to experience. The hotel itself is surrounded by water ponds and gives the impression of a floating paradise. This one of a kind resort is destined to give you a sense of Euphoria.
