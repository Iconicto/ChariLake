Attractions

Structure

Designed using exquisite timber and steel the resort is interconnected by timber bridges. Stroll on these beautifully designed bridges with the Koggala Lake in the backdrop. Elegantly built with airy open spaces, the structure in relentlessly graced and caressed with the soft and gentle breeze of the Koggala Lake.

Viewing Decks

Luxuriously and strategically designed viewing decks offer incredible sights to cherish while you enjoy a drink or just relax with the soothing breeze of the Koggala Lake in your hair.

Cuisine

Chari Lake offers a wide range of pallet scintillating cuisines ranging from traditional Sri Lankan dishes to Thai, Chinese and European diversity. Fish directly sourced from the lake is a treat to experience. The well-equipped and modern kitchen is open for viewing, hence watch your meals being prepared. Experience some of the great customary local dishes that are exclusively prepared for you.

Rooms

Chari Lake consists of 10 rooms and two suites. Accommodation is spaciously built and elegantly crafted. An exquisitely designed interior offers a sense of relaxation, and is certain to put you in the right mood. Large private balconies offer a wonderful view of the Koggala lake and its surroundings. The ambiance is perfect for a relaxing nap or to kick your heels up and read that favorite novel of your choice. Teak masterpieces of furniture adorn the room to give that classy finish it richly deserves.

Fruits

Indulge in the nourishing fruits of this tropical island that grow right around the resort.
